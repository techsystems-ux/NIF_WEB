Learning Overview

What You Learn In The Interior Designing Programme

From space planning to portfolio development, our curriculum is built to give you real skills that translate directly into professional work.

Space Planning

Students learn to analyse and optimise floor plans for residential, commercial and hospitality spaces, balancing function with aesthetics.

Materials & Finishes

Students study flooring, wall finishes, furniture materials, and surface treatments to make informed, industry-standard specification decisions.

AutoCAD & 3D Modelling

Technical skills in AutoCAD drafting, SketchUp 3D modelling, and photorealistic rendering to communicate designs with precision.

Lighting Design

Principles of natural and artificial lighting, fixture selection, and how light shapes the mood and usability of any interior space.

Interior Styling

Furniture layout, accessory curation, colour palettes, and the art of presenting a cohesive, client-ready interior concept.

Portfolio Development

By graduation every student leaves with a professional portfolio of real design projects that speaks for itself to employers and clients.
