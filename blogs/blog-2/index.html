Fashion Week

NIF Global Kondhwa, Pune @ New York Fashion Week

March 5, 2025 4 min read

New York Fashion Week is a global hub for style, innovation, and creativity, showcasing the best in fashion from around the world. NIF Global Kondhwa is proud to be part of this prestigious global fashion calendar, connecting its students and faculty with one of the most exciting platforms in the fashion industry.

Where Creativity Meets the Global Stage

New York Fashion Week (NYFW), held biannually in February and September, is one of the "Big Four" fashion weeks alongside London, Milan, and Paris. It attracts the world's top designers, models, buyers, and media — and now, NIF Global Kondhwa has made its mark on this celebrated stage.

Students and faculty from NIF Global Kondhwa attended and participated in key events during NYFW, gaining first-hand exposure to international design trends, networking with global industry professionals, and showcasing the calibre of talent emerging from India's design education sector.

What the Experience Meant for Students

For NIF Global students, attending New York Fashion Week was transformative. They witnessed live runway shows, met established designers, visited showrooms, and understood the logistics behind major fashion productions. This kind of real-world exposure is central to NIF Global Kondhwa's philosophy of education — learning beyond the classroom.

"Being at NYFW gave me a completely different perspective on what fashion design means at a global scale," said one of the participating students. "It pushed me to think bigger about my career and my designs."

NIF Global's Commitment to Global Exposure

NIF Global Kondhwa regularly facilitates participation in global fashion events, industry workshops, and international study tours. These opportunities allow students to benchmark themselves against international standards, build global networks, and understand the business of fashion at its highest level.

The New York Fashion Week collaboration is a testament to NIF Global's standing as a premier design education institution — one that opens doors not just to a career, but to the world.
