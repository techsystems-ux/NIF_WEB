Ananya Panday Launches NIF Global In India

November 13, 2024 5 min read

Mumbai, November 13, 2024 – Bollywood superstar and Style icon Ananya Panday launched the New York Institute of Fashion (NIF Global) in India at NCPA, Mumbai. As the brand's Style Icon, Ananya engaged and inspired students from NIF Global centers across Mumbai and other parts of India through an exclusive master session. During the session, she shared valuable insights on personal style, individuality, and the evolving world of fashion.

An Inspiring Master Session

Mr. Anil Shinde, Centre Director of NIF Global Kondhwa, Pune, stated that the event was highly impactful, offering students an opportunity to gain valuable industry knowledge and real-world insights. Students came away feeling motivated, inspired, and more connected to the fashion world they aspire to be part of.

About NIF Global

Headquartered in Manhattan, New York, NIF Global blends an internationally inspired curriculum with India's vibrant creative culture. The institute is mentored by renowned industry legends such as Manish Malhotra, Ashley Rebello, Twinkle Khanna and Gauri Khan, offering students a unique platform to learn from some of the most influential names in fashion and design.

NIF Global Kondhwa continues to grow as a hub for creative talent and design excellence in Pune, and this high-profile launch is just one of many milestones in its journey towards shaping India's design landscape.
