Courses & Duration

Fashion Design Courses Offered

Learn, explore, and stay inspired with expert-written articles.

BDes In Fashion Design

Duration: 4 Years

This is a comprehensive undergraduate program for students who want in depth learning in fashion design. The course focuses on design thinking, creative development, and technical skills. Students work on detailed projects and build a strong portfolio over time. The program is suited for those looking for a long term career in fashion design with strong fundamentals.

BVoc In Fashion Design

Duration: 3 Years

This undergraduate program focuses on practical skill development. Students learn design basics along with studio practice and software training. The course includes industry exposure to help students understand real work environments. It is suitable for students who want hands on learning with career focused outcomes.

MVoc In Fashion Design

Duration: 2 Years

This is a postgraduate program designed for advanced skill development. The course helps students specialise further in fashion design. Learning focuses on professional growth and applied design work. It is suitable for students who want to strengthen their expertise and industry readiness.

MDes In Fashion Design

Duration: 4 Years

The MDes program is designed for designers aiming to elevate their creative and professional skills. The course focuses on advanced concepts, innovation, and leadership in design. Students work on high level projects and refine their individual design approach. It is suited for those planning senior or specialised roles in the fashion industry.

Fashion Design

What You Learn In The Fashion Design Program

Explore essential design concepts, practical skills, and industry-relevant tools that prepare you for professional fashion design projects and creative careers.

Design Fundamentals

Students begin by learning the core principles of design including elements, color theory, composition, and visual communication. This builds a strong creative foundation for all future work.

Fabrics And Textiles

Students learn about different types of fabrics and materials. They understand how fabrics behave and how to choose the right material for a design. This knowledge is important for both creativity and functionality.

Garment Construction And Techniques

From pattern making to draping and stitching, students learn the technical process of turning a design into a finished garment. Hands-on practice ensures strong construction skills.

Fashion Illustration And Concept Development

Students develop the ability to sketch and illustrate fashion ideas clearly. This skill is essential for communicating design concepts to teams, clients, and manufacturers.

Styling And Presentation

Students learn how to style outfits and present collections effectively. This includes mood boards, lookbooks, and professional presentation techniques used in the fashion industry.

Portfolio Development

Throughout the program, students build a professional portfolio that showcases their best work. A strong portfolio is critical for job applications, interviews, and freelance opportunities.

Students Looking For A Professional Career

Work independently on design projects, collaborations, or custom orders. Freelancing offers flexibility and creative freedom.
