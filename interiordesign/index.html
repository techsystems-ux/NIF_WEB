Frequently Asked Questions

What educational qualification is mandatory for admission at NIF Pune Kondhwa ?

Admission to NIF Pune Kondhwa requires at least a 10th or 12th grade for certificate and diploma courses, 12th grade for undergraduate courses (B.Voc, B.Des, BBA), and a bachelor's degree for postgraduate courses (M.Voc). Always verify with the institution for the latest details.

Do the courses at NIF Pune Kondhwa provide industry exposure to the students ?

Yes, the courses at NIF Pune Kondhwa provide industry exposure to students. The institute offers internships, workshops, live projects, and collaborations with industry professionals, helping students gain practical experience and connect with the interior design industry.

Does NIF Pune Kondhwa provide internships ?

Yes. Students get structured internship placements in their 2nd and 3rd year with leading brands. Past students have interned at Tommy Hilfiger, Myntra, and Royal Interiors Interiors. Over 85% of our graduates are placed within 6 months of completing their course.

Will the faculty members at NIF Pune Kondhwa help me build my portfolio ?

Yes, the faculty members at NIF Pune Kondhwa actively assist students in building their portfolios. They provide guidance and mentorship, helping students showcase their skills through projects, assignments, and portfolio development, which is crucial for their future career in the interior design industry.

How can I get more information about NIF Pune Kondhwa ?

To get more information about NIF Pune Kondhwa, you can visit their official website for details on courses and admissions, contact the admissions office for specific queries, follow their social media for updates, or attend webinars and campus visits to interact with faculty and students.

What are the fees for the courses?

Our fee structure is highly competitive and varies by the duration (2, 3, or 4 years). Please submit an enquiry to receive the exact fee breakdown.

What is the duration of the programs?

We offer flexible 2-Year, 3-Year, and 4-Year specialized programs in Interior Design.

Who is eligible to apply?

Anyone who has completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) in any stream is eligible to apply for our foundational degree programs.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We provide 100% placement assistance. Our alumni work with top brands like Aditya Birla, Reliance Brands, and more.
