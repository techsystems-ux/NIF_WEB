Learning Overview

What You Learn In The Fashion Designing Programme

From design theory to hands-on garment construction, our curriculum is built to give you real skills that translate directly into professional work.

Design Fundamentals

Students begin with core principles including colour theory, composition, and visual communication — the creative foundation for all future design work.

Fabrics And Textiles

Students learn about different materials, how fabrics behave, and how to select the right textile for every design brief and context.

Garment Construction

Pattern making, draping, and stitching — students learn the complete technical process of turning a design concept into a finished garment.

Fashion Illustration

Students develop the ability to sketch and illustrate fashion ideas clearly — an essential skill for communicating designs to teams, clients, and manufacturers.

Styling And Presentation

Mood boards, lookbooks, and presentation techniques are all taught so students can present their work with confidence and professionalism.

Portfolio Development

By graduation, every student leaves with a strong, industry-ready portfolio — the most important tool for entering the professional fashion world.
