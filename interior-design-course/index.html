Courses & Duration

Interior Design Courses Offered

Learn, explore, and stay inspired with expert-written articles.

BDes In Interior Design

Duration: 4 Years

The BDes program is designed for students who want deep understanding of interior design. Learning focuses on space planning, design thinking, and system based approaches. Students work on detailed projects and gradually build strong design foundations. The course also supports portfolio development over time.

BVoc In Interior Design

Duration: 3 Years

The BVoc program combines design basics with hands on studio work. Students learn practical interior design skills along with essential software training. Industry exposure is introduced to help students understand how interior projects are handled in real work settings. This program suits students who want applied learning.

MVoc In Interior Design

Duration: 2 Years

The MVoc program is meant for advanced learning and professional growth. Students focus on specialised interior design skills and practical application. The course supports deeper understanding of execution, coordination, and leadership within design projects.

MDes In Interior Design

Duration: 4 Years

The MDes program is designed for professionals aiming to refine their design thinking. Learning focuses on advanced concepts, innovation, and detailed project work. Students develop a stronger design perspective and prepare for leadership roles in interior design.

Interior Design

Who Should Choose This Interior Design Course

Explore essential design concepts, practical skills, and industry-relevant tools that prepare you for professional interior design projects and creative careers.

Interior Design Foundations

Students begin with core design principles including elements of design, colour theory, and spatial awareness. This builds a strong creative foundation for all interior work.

Space Planning And Layout

Students learn how to plan and organise spaces efficiently. This includes understanding circulation, zoning, and functionality in residential and commercial interiors.

Materials And Finishes

Students study different materials, textures, and finishes used in interiors. Understanding material properties helps in making informed design and specification decisions.

Technical Drawings And Drafting

From floor plans to elevations and sections, students learn technical drawing skills essential for communicating design ideas to clients and contractors.

Furniture, Lighting, And Detailing

Students explore furniture design, lighting techniques, and interior detailing. These elements bring spaces to life and define the character of an interior.

Styling And Portfolio Development

Throughout the program, students build a professional portfolio that showcases their best interior projects. A strong portfolio is critical for job applications and freelance opportunities.
