Welcome To NIF Kondhwa, Pune

Learn Design The Practical Way At NIF Global Kondhwa, A Leading Design Institute In Pune

NIF Global Kondhwa, Pune is a leading design institute offering comprehensive programs in fashion and interior design. Formerly known as NIFD Global.

Over the years, NIF Global Kondhwa has established a focused learning environment that emphasises practical application, design thinking, and conceptual clarity. The curriculum is designed to help students develop discipline, patience, and continuous improvement through project-based learning.

Why Choose Us
